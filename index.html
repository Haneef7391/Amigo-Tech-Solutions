Get to Know Us

Amigo Tech Solutions (ATS) was established in June 2022 in Anaparthi with a commitment to delivering top-quality computer repair and IT solutions. Our expertise spans across hardware diagnostics, software installations, printer repairs, and essential stationery services. Beyond traditional repairs, we offer advanced IT solutions, including CCTV installation & maintenance, gaming PC setup, bulk computer setup for businesses, and remote tech support. Whether you're facing a faulty computer, network issues, or need a custom gaming build, our team provides fast, reliable, and efficient services. Our mission is to keep your devices running smoothly, ensuring minimal downtime and uninterrupted work. We also help aspiring content creators with YouTube and social media tech content, guiding them in recording, editing, and unboxing reviews.
